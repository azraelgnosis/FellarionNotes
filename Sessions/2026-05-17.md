## The Next Day
### Issa be Snoopin'
- While snooping around near [[Trajedie]] and [[AnnieLashawn]]'s wagon, [[Issa]] discovers a new "grocery" list, like the last one minus Adult [[Sea Elf]] and Adult [[Wood Elf]] but now including Adult [[Orc]] Female.
- [[AnnieLashawn]] apprehends [[Issa]] as he is snooping. [[Trajedie]] takes the stack of papers and walks away. [[AnnieLashawn]] invites [[Issa]] into their wagon or out of camp for a "talk".
- They pas by [[Cai Ap Namara]] along their way. [[Issa]] tells [[Cai Ap Namara]] to tell the others! [[Cai Ap Namara]] announces to the group that [[Issa]] is preparing to get laid. Shenanigans ensue!
