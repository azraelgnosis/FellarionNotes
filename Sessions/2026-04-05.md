## At the Ruins
- The party arrives at the Ruins to the south of [[Roumpīni]]
- One of the Topaz Cultists is dangling "fake Palug" over a cliff while a second is talking to [[Taliesen]], [[Hyacinthus]]. [[Dike]] is hiding behind some cover in wait.
- [[Issa]] attempts to casts [[Prestidigitation]] to pretend that he might trade it for "Fake Palug"; they don't buy it.
- In short order, the two cultists are dispatched. "Real [[Palug]]" removes the magical collar and the "Fake Palug" transforms into a [[Cave Elf|Cave]] [[Elf]] wearing [[Palug]]'s clothing.
- The cultists assert that the [[Quartz]] has been taking somewhere far away.
