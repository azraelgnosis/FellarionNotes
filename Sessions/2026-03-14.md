- [[AnnieLashawn]] catches up to [[Pryderi ap Rhiannon|Mabrian]] in short order, directing him to stop giving chase after [[Palug]]. [[Pryderi ap Rhiannon|Mabrian]] refuses and continues to run, almost randomly, through the city.
- [[Trajedie]] starts the wagon headed towards the inn the cultists mentioned.
- [[Pryderi ap Rhiannon|Mabrian]] sees a [[Charybdis|Topaz-laden waif]], and attempts to tackle her. She nimbly dodges out of the way and runs off, [[Pryderi ap Rhiannon|Mabrian]] in hot pursuit.
